Welcome to the Interactive Museum of Gaming and Puzzlery

We are now members of the Wizards Play Network!
Check out our calendar to see all the new Magic: The Gathering and
Dungeons & Dragons events we have scheduled.

The Interactive Museum of Gaming and Puzzlery celebrates the diverse beauty of structured play and its components. We gather games of all sorts and share them with all the people who love to play.

Daily admission fee:
$5 for adults
$4 for senior citizens
$3 for minors
free for kids under 6
